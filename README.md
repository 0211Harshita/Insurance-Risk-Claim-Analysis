📊 Insurance Risk & Claim Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

This project presents an interactive Insurance Risk & Claim Analysis Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze policy distribution, claim behavior, and customer risk patterns. The dashboard transforms raw insurance data into actionable business insights through advanced data modeling, DAX calculations, and dynamic visualizations.

🚀 Project Overview
The goal of this project is to help stakeholders understand claim trends, policy performance, customer demographics, and risk distribution to support data-driven decision-making in the insurance domain.

🛠️ Tools & Technologies
Power BI
Power Query – Data cleaning, transformation, and shaping
Data Modeling – Relationship building between multiple tables
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – KPI creation and advanced measures
Interactive Visualizations & Slicers

📌 Key Features
KPI cards showing:
Total Policies
Total Claim Amount
Average Claim Frequency
Average Claim Amount

Policy analysis by:
Car Use (Commercial vs Private)
Car Make & Manufacturing Year
Coverage Zone
Age Group & Gender

Customer risk insights based on:
Education level
Marital status
Kids driving factor

Dynamic measure selection for flexible analysis
Fully interactive filtering for deeper exploration

📈 Business Insights Delivered
Identification of high-risk customer segments
Claim distribution trends across demographics
Policy concentration across regions and vehicle categories
Data-driven understanding of insurance exposure and claim patterns.
